## Project Description

ELA-1 Voice Assistant:

ELA-1 is an intelligent voice assistant created by GeekCoder55 as a Python learning project. It is designed to provide a wide range of functionalities and assist users in performing various tasks through voice commands.

Key Features:

Voice Recognition: ELA-1 leverages advanced speech recognition technology to accurately understand and interpret user commands.
Natural Language Processing: The voice assistant utilizes natural language processing techniques to comprehend user queries and generate appropriate responses.
Task Automation: ELA-1 can automate routine tasks such as opening youtube, gmail, google or search wikipedia, or play video on youtube allowing users to be more efficient and productive.
Modularity: The project follows a modular design, making it easy to add new features and extend its capabilities with additional functionality.


Installation:

To install and run ELA-1 on your local machine, follow these steps:

Usage:

After successfully setting up ELA-1, you can interact with the voice assistant using voice commands. 

WHO ARE YOU: Ela will introduce herself

OPEN GOOGLE: she  Open google on new tab

OPEN GMAIL: she  Open gmail on new tab

OPEN YOUTUBE: open youtube on new tab

OPEN TWITCH: open twitch on new tab

OPEN MEET: open google meet on new tab

SEARCH: search any website on internet by its perfect address  (Play "web address here")
example: search "facebook.com"  (Note: u need to tell the exact address like .com in this case its not just search"facebook" instead its search"facebook.com")
example 2: search "amazon.com" (need full address not just "amazon")
(note: here after saying "SEARCH" wait for Ela to confirm, it will speak the responce like "web address please" and then request for any website)


PLAY: play anything on "youtube specifically"  (Play "your request on youtube")
example: play "dinamyte by bts"  (wait for some seconds then it will run the song)

WIKIPEDIA: "according to wikipedia" (if u wanna search wikipedia then use this command)
example: "who is abraham lincoln according to wikipedia" or "according to wikipedia who is elon musk"

TIME: just say time and ela will tell the time
example: simply say "time"

GOOD: u can commend ELA for her hardwork :)
